International Collaborations – Research, tech hubs, and exchange programs may accept foreign participants. Diplomatic/NGO Channels – Embassies and global NGOs can link non-Rwandans to local aid. Special Cases – Private charities may assist vulnerable foreigners (e.g., trafficking survivors). How to Seek Help? Contact the project directly. Ese umushinga waguhaye ubufasha ugishobora kukwiguriza amafaranga nyuma yo kurangira?

Ndumva ushaka kumenya niba umushinga w’ubufasha, umaze kurangira, ushobora kuba ugifite uburenganzira bwo gusaba cyangwa guhabwa amafaranga nyuma y’uko uwo mushinga urangije. Ibisubizo bihinduka bitewe n’uko ibintu byanditse muri ayo masezerano n’amabwiriza y’umushinga.

Biterwa n’amasezerano n’amabwiriza

Niba hari amasezerano yanditse (nka MOU, contract, cyangwa politiki y’umushinga) yavugaga ko hari amafaranga cyangwa inkunga izatangwa nyuma y’igihe, ayo masezerano niyo akurikizwa. Ariko niba nta masezerano yanditse ahari, akenshi nta muntu uba ugomba kwishyurwa nyuma y’uko umushinga urangije.

Itandukaniro hagati y’umukozi n’umufashwa (beneficiary)

Iyo umuntu yari umukozi w’umushinga (yakoraga akazi ahabwa umushahara), ashobora kugira uburenganzira ku mishahara ya nyuma, indembe cyangwa andi mafaranga bitewe n’amasezerano n’amategeko y’akazi. Ariko iyo umuntu yari umufashwa (yakiraga inkunga cyangwa serivisi), akenshi ntibamwishyura amafaranga nyuma y’uko umushinga urangije—uretse niba hari ibyo bemeranyije byihariye.

Inkunga zifatika n’ibivugwa mu masezerano

Hari igihe umushinga utanga ibintu birambye (nko imashini, igishoro, ubwishingizi cyangwa ubutaka). Niba byari byavuzwe ko ibyo bintu bizasigara ku mufashwa, bizasigara. Niba ari inguzanyo, hari amategeko n’imiterere y’inguzanyo agena niba igomba kwishyurwa.

Icyo wakora kugirango umenye neza

Saba kopi y’amasezerano cyangwa inyandiko zerekana uko inkunga yagenewe. Vugana n’abayobozi b’umushinga usabe ibisobanuro byanditse. Niba ari ikibazo cy’umushahara, shaka inama mu kigo gishinzwe abakozi (labour office) cyangwa umwunganizi mu by’amategeko.

Aho ushobora gusaba ubufasha

Ushobora kwegera abayobozi b’umushinga, umukozi ushinzwe abakiriya/beneficiaries, ubuyobozi bw’umudugudu cyangwa akarere, ndetse n’imiryango yunganira abaturage mu by’amategeko cyangwa amashyirahamwe y’abafatanyabikorwa (NGO forum) kugira ngo bagufashe gusobanukirwa no gukemura ikibazo.

Icyitonderwa: Niba wifuza, nakugenera urugero rw’inyandiko (letter/email) yo gusaba ibisobanuro ku mushinga, cyangwa nkagufasha kuyitunganya mu Kinyarwanda cyangwa mu rundi rurimi. Mbwira icyo wifuza.

Amakosa Ashobora Gutuma Umwana Avanwaho mu Mushinga w'Ubufasha

Niba umwana ari mu mushinga ufasha imbabare (nk’aho ahabwa inkunga, ubufasha, cyangwa kwitabwaho), hari amakosa ashobora gutuma bamukuramo cyangwa bamuhagarika muri uwo mushinga. Aha hasi hari urutonde rw'ibyo bakunze kubona nk'ibigomba gukosorwa cyangwa impamvu zishobora gutuma bafata icyemezo cyo kumukuraho.

  1. Kwitwara nabi cyane ku bandi — gusuzugura, gutuka, cyangwa gukubita abandi bari muri uwo mushinga.
  2. Kwangiza ibintu by’umushinga — gusenya, kwiba, cyangwa kurangaza ibintu bahawe kugira ngo bifashe bose.
  3. Gukora ubujura — kwiba ku bandi bana cyangwa ku bantu bakorana n’umushinga.
  4. Kutubahiriza amategeko y’umushinga — nko gutoroka ibikorwa, kutitabira ku bushake, cyangwa guhora arwanya abayobozi.
  5. Gukoresha ibiyobyabwenge cyangwa inzoga — cyane cyane iyo bihabanye n’amategeko n’amabwiriza y’umushinga.
  6. Kujya mu bikorwa by’ubugizi bwa nabi — urugero kwifatanya n’imitwe y’abajura cyangwa ibikorwa by’urugomo.
  7. Kubeshya cyangwa guhimba inkuru — gutanga amakuru atari yo ku byerekeye ubuzima bwe cyangwa ibikenerwa.
  8. Kutia ku burere — gukora ibikomeretsa abandi bana cyangwa abakozi, cyangwa kwirengagiza amategeko y'uburere.

Ingamba: Muri rusange, mishinga myinshi ibanza kugerageza guhugura no gukosora umwana mbere yo kumwirukana. Ariko iyo amakosa akomeje cyangwa ari akomeye cyane, bafata icyemezo cyo kumuhagarika kugira ngo barinde abandi banyamuryango n'umutekano w'umushinga.
